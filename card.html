Sunny

First registered by the AKC in 1904, the Chihuahua is one of the oldest dog breeds on the American continent and one of the smallest breeds in the world.

  • Defence: Snake catcher
  • Service: Guard dog, Companion dog
  • Longevity: 14 - 18 years
  • Intelligence: Police sniffer dog

Unfortunatelly, this little dog has bad reputation. Usually untrained, these little dogs end up being spoiled - by the owner. So, the bad reputation should go to the owners, not the dog.
